Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

Welcome to College Connect ("we," "our," or "us"). We are fully dedicated to protecting your personal information and maintaining your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how your personal datasets are compiled, structuralized, and secured when you access our platform or engage with our matching network systems.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a secure peer network mapping layout across institutional tracks, we process the following standard data vectors:

  • Account Profile Parameters: Full name, institutional or personal email addresses, phone contacts, and educational curriculum tracks.
  • Academic Credentials (Seniors): Verifiable identity details used to authenticate your active "Old Student" campus profile tier.
  • Communication Trails: Encryption-shielded textual data logs sent back and forth within our peer chat interface environments.

2. Operational Data Processing

Your information is systematically limited to core performance functions including:

3. Gateway & Transaction Safety

All user connection fees are handled via our integration with Razorpay payment gateway systems. Your private financial tokens, banking details, or card signatures are processed natively inside a 256-bit secure SSL tunnel managed exclusively by Razorpay. Our platform servers never store, log, or observe your core financial credentials.

4. Sharing & Data Boundaries

We do not sell, barter, or distribute your private profiles to advertising nodes or third-party syndicates. Your data profile factors are exclusively shared with fellow verified users inside your targeted college lane when matching handshakes are actively locked in.

5. Administrative Protections

Our platform framework uses security tokens, automated script validation flags, and active CSRF structural filters to block unauthorized accounts from harvesting your data arrays or manipulating profile structures.

6. Policy Questions & Rights

You hold total administrative management control over your information profiles. If you wish to pull down your student history records, wipe chat data parameters from our backend logs, or modify structural identity errors, please file an escalation directly at our Support Desk Channel or reach our compliance system controllers at collegeconnect@collegeco.in.
